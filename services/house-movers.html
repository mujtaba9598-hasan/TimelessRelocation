FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our house moving services in Dubai.

Do you offer same-day house moving in Dubai?
Yes, we offer same-day house moving services subject to availability. For urgent moves, contact us on WhatsApp and we will do our best to accommodate your timeline, even on short notice.
Can you move me from an apartment with no lift access?
Absolutely. Our team is experienced in navigating stairs, narrow corridors, and walk-up buildings. We use specialised equipment like trolleys and straps to safely move items through tight spaces without causing damage.
Do you handle building management and NOC requirements?
Yes, we are familiar with building management requirements across Dubai. We can guide you through the NOC process and coordinate with building security to ensure smooth access for both pickup and delivery.
What happens if something gets damaged during the move?
All our moves are carefully handled. In the rare event of damage, we have a straightforward claims process. Our team documents the condition of items before and after the move for complete transparency.
How long does a typical house move take?
A studio or 1-bedroom apartment typically takes 3-5 hours. A 2-3 bedroom flat or house usually takes a full day. Larger properties may require two days. We give you an accurate timeline during the free survey.
