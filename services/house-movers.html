Professional House Movers in Dubai

Residential Moving

Apartment, Flat, and House Moving Specialists

Timeless Relocation provides reliable house moving services across Dubai, tailored to the size of your home. Whether you are moving from a studio in Dubai Marina or a family apartment in Downtown Dubai, we match the right process to your move so you get accurate pricing and a smooth experience.

Small Moves (Studio & 1-Bedroom)

No physical site visit needed. Simply send us photos of your items and space via WhatsApp. Our team reviews the photos and provides a transparent quote. Once approved, we handle everything: disassemble furniture, pack securely, load, transport, unload, and reassemble or fix items at your new home.

Large Moves (2+ Bedrooms)

For larger homes, we conduct a mandatory on-site visit to assess the volume of belongings and property layout. All service details and additional charges are discussed face-to-face during the visit, with no surprise costs. Full furniture disassembly and reassembly is included as standard.

Handyman Services

Beyond the move itself, we offer handyman services for an additional charge: wall-mounting TVs, fixing shelves, hanging curtains, mounting clocks and pictures, and more. Furniture assembly and fixing is always part of the standard job. Our team makes sure everything is set up and functional in your new home.

Need a bigger property moved? Check out our villa moving service. For office relocations, see our commercial relocation page. Temporary storage is also available through our storage services.
