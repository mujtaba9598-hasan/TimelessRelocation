Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our movers and packers services in Dubai.

How much do movers and packers cost in Dubai?
The cost depends on the volume of items, distance, and special requirements. We offer free, no-obligation surveys and transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Contact us on WhatsApp for an instant quote tailored to your move.
Do you provide packing materials?
Yes, all packing materials are included in our service. We supply premium cartons, bubble wrap, stretch film, packing paper, heavy-duty tape, and custom wooden crating for fragile or oversized items.
How far in advance should I book movers in Dubai?
We recommend booking at least 3 to 5 days in advance, though we can accommodate urgent and same-day moves depending on availability. Peak season during summer months may require earlier booking.
Is my move insured with Timeless Relocation?
Yes, all moves with Timeless Relocation are carefully handled. We carry careful handling processes and protective packing that safeguard your belongings from pickup to delivery, giving you complete peace of mind throughout the process.
Do you move to other emirates outside Dubai?
Absolutely. We cover all seven UAE emirates including Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Al Ain, Ras al-Khaimah, Umm al-Quwain, and Fujairah. We also handle international relocations across the globe.
