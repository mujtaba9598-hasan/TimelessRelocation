How It Works

Our 4-Step Commercial Moving Process

1

Site Assessment

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our commercial relocation services in Dubai.

Can you move our office over a weekend to avoid business disruption?
Yes, weekend and after-hours office moves are our speciality. We plan the entire relocation around your business schedule so your team can leave on Friday and return to a fully set up office on Sunday or Monday.
How do you handle IT equipment and servers during a move?
Our IT-trained crew safely disconnects, labels, packs, and reconnects all computers, servers, networking gear, and peripherals. We use anti-static wrapping and padded cases to protect sensitive electronics.
Do you provide commercial moves in free zones like DIFC and DMCC?
Yes, we serve all Dubai free zones including DIFC, DMCC, Dubai Internet City, Dubai Media City, JAFZA, and Dubai Silicon Oasis, as well as mainland commercial areas like Business Bay and Sheikh Zayed Road.
How far in advance should we book a commercial relocation?
For small offices of up to 20 workstations, 1 to 2 weeks notice is usually sufficient. Larger corporate moves benefit from 3 to 4 weeks of planning time. Contact us as early as possible for the best scheduling options.
Is our office equipment protected during the move?
Yes, all commercial moves include careful protective handling covering furniture, IT equipment, and documents. We can arrange additional protective measures for high-value items upon request.
