FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our storage services in Dubai.

What types of items can I store with Timeless Relocation?
You can store household furniture, personal belongings, office equipment, documents, artwork, antiques, seasonal items, and commercial inventory. We do not accept hazardous materials, perishable goods, or illegal items.
Are your storage facilities climate-controlled?
Yes, our storage units are climate-controlled to maintain optimal temperature and humidity levels. This is especially important in Dubai's heat for protecting wooden furniture, electronics, artwork, and sensitive documents.
What is the minimum storage period?
Our minimum storage period is one week. We offer flexible plans ranging from weekly to monthly to annual terms, so you only pay for the time you need. There are no long-term commitments required.
Is my stored property insured?
Yes, all items stored with Timeless Relocation are covered by comprehensive insurance. For high-value items such as artwork or antiques, we can arrange additional coverage to match the declared value.
Can you pick up and deliver my items to and from storage?
Absolutely. We provide full door-to-door service including professional packing, pickup, secure storage, and delivery back to any address in Dubai or across the UAE whenever you need your items returned.
