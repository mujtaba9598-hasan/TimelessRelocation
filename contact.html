FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our moving services in Dubai and across the UAE.

How much does it cost to hire movers in Dubai?
The cost depends on several factors including the size of your home, the volume of items, the distance, and any special handling requirements. A studio or one-bedroom apartment typically starts from AED 800, while a full villa move can range from AED 3,000 to AED 8,000 or more. We provide free, no-obligation quotes tailored to your specific needs. Contact us on WhatsApp at +971 56 865 4794 for an accurate estimate within minutes.
How far in advance should I book my move?
We recommend booking at least 3 to 5 days in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak season (end-of-month and summer months). However, we do our best to accommodate same-day and next-day requests based on availability. The earlier you book, the better we can plan and assign the right team and vehicles for your move.
Do you provide packing materials?
Yes, we provide all packing materials as part of our full-service moving packages. This includes high-quality cardboard boxes in multiple sizes, bubble wrap, stretch film, packing tape, tissue paper, wardrobe boxes for clothing, and custom crating for fragile or high-value items. If you prefer to pack yourself, we can supply materials separately at competitive rates.
How do you protect my belongings during the move?
All moves with Timeless Relocation include professional protective handling at every stage. Our trained team ensures your belongings are carefully packed, loaded, transported, and unloaded with the utmost care. For high-value items such as antiques, fine art, or expensive electronics, we offer additional protective measures. Our team will discuss all options during the quotation process.
Do you offer storage services along with moving?
Absolutely. We operate secure, climate-controlled storage facilities in Dubai for short-term and long-term needs. Whether you need to store furniture between moves, keep seasonal items safe, or hold business inventory, our storage solutions are flexible and affordable. All stored items are inventoried, wrapped, and kept in clean, pest-free units with 24/7 security and CCTV surveillance.
Which areas in the UAE do you serve?
Timeless Relocation serves all seven emirates of the UAE: Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Al Ain, Ras al-Khaimah, Umm al-Quwain, and Fujairah. We handle local moves within a single emirate, inter-emirate relocations, and international moves to destinations worldwide. No matter where you are moving, our team has the experience and logistics network to handle it efficiently.

Need Urgent Moving Help?

Our team is available seven days a week to assist with last-minute and emergency moves across Dubai and all UAE emirates. Call us now or send a WhatsApp message for an instant response and a free quote.
